Let's Do That Hockey

This site tells the story of profesional hockey over the years 1909 to 2011 using data from Kaggle's Proffesional Hockey Database.

The Hockey Database is a collection of historical statistics from men's professional hockey teams in North America.

Authors: Owen Bezick & Daniel Cowan
